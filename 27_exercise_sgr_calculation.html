Finding Your SGR: Summit Sports Inc.

Calculate the sustainable growth rate step by step

Company Data

Return on Equity (ROE) 16.8%
Dividend Payout Ratio 35%
Management's Target Growth Rate 15%
1
Calculate the Retention Ratio
The retention ratio is the portion of earnings kept in the business rather than paid as dividends.
Retention Ratio = 1 − Dividend Payout Ratio
2
Calculate Summit's Sustainable Growth Rate
Use the SGR formula to determine the maximum growth rate Summit can sustain without changing its financial policies.
SGR = ROE × Retention Ratio
3
Compare to Target Growth
Management wants to grow at 15%. Is this above or below the sustainable growth rate of 10.9%?
Target Growth: 15%
Sustainable Growth Rate: ~10.9%
Gap: 15% − 10.9% = 4.1 percentage points above SGR
Options to close the gap:
• Improve ROE: Increase profit margins, asset turnover, or leverage
• Increase retention: Reduce dividend payout (keep more earnings)
• Raise external financing: Issue debt or equity
• Slow growth target: Reduce from 15% to match SGR
