The financial system serves as a bridge between those who have surplus funds and those who need capital. Click on any of the three components above to learn more about their role.

This flow is essential for economic growth — it allows savings to be channeled into productive investments that create jobs, innovation, and value.

Key Insight: Finance as a System and a Signal

System: Finance channels capital across the economy — from savers to firms — supporting investment, operations, and growth.

Signal: Interest rates, market prices, and risk premiums reflect collective expectations about the future. Financial managers must read these signals to time decisions and manage risk effectively.
